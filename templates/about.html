Our Mission

At CUTM Result Portal, we strive to simplify the process of accessing and managing academic records. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for students to access their semester grades effortlessly, empowering them to make informed decisions about their education.

By offering accurate grade calculation, easy access to academic records, and robust administrative tools, we aim to streamline the academic experience for students and administrators alike.

Why Choose Us

Accuracy: We ensure precise calculation of SGPA and CGPA, providing reliable academic data.

User-Friendly Interface: Our intuitive interface makes it easy to navigate and access the information you need.

Security: We prioritize the security of user data, implementing robust measures to safeguard sensitive information.

Furthermore, this website offers the advantage of providing accurate CGPA and SGPA calculations even before the official release on the college's ERP system. So there is no need to wait for the ERP's official release to view their original CGPA and SGPA. This enables students to access their academic standing promptly and plan their educational journey effectively.
